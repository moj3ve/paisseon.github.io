Features

  • The only open source IAP cracker ever made for iOS
  • Compatible with all devices on iOS 13-14
  • Fast and lightweight because it only injects into the apps you choose
  • Option to fake receipts, which lets you hack more apps

Known Issues

  • Receipt faker doesn't work on iOS 14 and I'm not good enough to fix it (yet?)
  • Server sided purchases, including most subscriptions, are incompatible with Satella
